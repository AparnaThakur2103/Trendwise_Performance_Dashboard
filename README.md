## 🧭 What This Dashboard Solves  

- Makes short-term performance shifts clearly visible  
- Helps identify when workload spikes vs when it stabilizes  
- Gives a quick snapshot of how productivity and accuracy behave together  

---

## ⚡ Why It Matters  

- Performance is not static — trends reveal hidden patterns  
- Even small spikes can impact efficiency and workload balance  
- Consistent accuracy under fluctuating volume indicates process stability  

---

## 🔬 What I Focused On  

- Keeping the dashboard simple but insight-driven  
- Prioritizing trend clarity over excessive visuals  
- Ensuring metrics (like APH) reflect real performance, not misleading averages  

---

## 🚀 What Can Be Extended  

- Add deeper drill-down views for specific time periods  
- Introduce anomaly detection for sudden spikes  
- Connect with live data for continuous monitoring  
- Enhance comparisons across different performance dimensions
